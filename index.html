I am Shyanne Castillo, a student at UC Riverside. I am currently in my second year in the undergraduate psychology department. I have always had an acute interest int he workings of the human psyche, with my research in the affects social media have on young minds int he United States. This research has led me to believe that the harmful effects of social media has a negative impact on the envionment of the work place. I've discovered that there is a lack of awareness, loss of attention span, and a nagative boss-employee relationship. This research is only the beginning of a whole new world of acedemia in the world of psychology.

Another one of my research ventures is my study on human-pet relationships and its affect on our psyche. This is a very inder researched area that I have had alot of experience with. I discivered that there is a high positive correlation between owning a pet and productivity in the work place. We surveyed over 500 pet owners and 500 non-pet owners and found that those with pets reported a significantly higher productivity rating than those who did not own pets. Pet owners reported an average of 7/10 on their productivity levels and those without pets reported an everage of 6.2/10 productivity levels. This led to my conclusion that not only are pets good for social relaitons, they are also good for work relations!

In my quest to find more ways for people to get on in the work place, I found that women find it 2x harder to communicate with male superiors. In an interview with 500 women in corporate jobs, I discoevered 350/500 of them found it difficult to voice an opinion to males in charge. Comparing this to our interviews with males in the same positions as the women, we reported that 125/500 found it difficult to communicate with their male superiors, leading me to believe that there is still a misbalance in power between men and women in the work place. Understanding this fact is a set towards building a future where there is a sense of equality between men and women in the work place.
